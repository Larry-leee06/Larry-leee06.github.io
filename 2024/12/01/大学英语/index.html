翻译

1:孔子是中国历史上著名的思想家，教育家，是儒家学派的创始人

Confucins is a famous thinker,an educator in Chinese history and the founder

of confucianism.

2:《论语》(The Analects)收录了孔子的思想，它是中国文化的经典著作(classic)，对后来历代思想家和文学家产生了很大影响

The Analects contains the thoughts of confucius.It is a classic work of

ancient Chinese culture and has had a great influence on thinkers and

writers of later dynasties.

3:如今，孔子的学说不仅受到中国人的重视，也越来越得到整个国际社会的关注

Nowdays,the teachings of confucins are not only valued by the Chinese people,

but also increasingly gaining attention from the entire international communi

ty.

4:孝道(filial piety)是中国古代社会的基本道德规范(code of ethics)之一

Filial piety is one of the basic code of ethics in ancient Chinese society.

5:一般来说，孝道指子女对父母应尽的义务，主要包括尊敬，关爱及赡养老人

In general,filial piety refers to the duties that children should fuifill

towards their parents,mainly including respect,care,and support for the

elderly.

6:孝道是中国社会千百年来维系家庭关系的道德准则。它毫无疑问是中华民族的一种传统美德

Filial piety is the moral standard that has maintained family relationships

in Chinese society for thousands of years.It is undoubtedly virtue of the

Chinese nation.

7:郑和1371年出生于云南昆明,他不仅是明朝的外交家(diplomat),也是中国历史上最著名的海上探险家(maritime explorer)

Zheng He was born in Kunming,Yunnan in 1371.He was not only a diplomat in

the Ming Dynasty,but also the moet famous maritime explorer in Chinese

history.

8:从公元1405年起的28年间,郑和带领船队七下西洋(the western seas),出海的人员共计10万人,访问了30多个国家和地区

During the 28 years from 1405,zheng he led his fleet to the western seas

for seven times with more than 100000 people at sea and visited more

than 30 countries and regions.

9.郑和下西洋是世界航海(navigation)史上的壮举，加强了明朝和海外各国之间的关系

Zheng he’s voyages to the western seas were a great feat in the world’s

navigation history,strengthening the relationships between the Ming Dynasty

and the overseas countries.

10:随着互联网技术的发展，数字化教育使人们得以通过互联网进入虚拟教室，并随时随地学习

With the development of internet technology,digital education enables people

to enter virtual classrooms via the Internet and study anytime and anywhere.

11:数字化教育拓展了学习者学习的时间和空间，为终生学习提供了更多的可能性

Digital education expands the time and spare for learners to study and

provides more possibilities for lifelong learning.

12:中国将坚持教育优先发展，推进教育数字化，建设学习型社会，学习型大国

China will adhere to giving priority to the development of education, promote

digitalization of education, and build a learning society and a major learning

country.

13:新中国成立后,中国坚持独立自主的和平外交政策,在外交方面取得了巨大成就。截至2022年,中国已与181个国家建立了外交关系

Since the founding of the People’s Republic of China, China has persisted in

an independent foreign policy of peace and made great achievements in

diplomacy. As of 2022, China has established diplomatic relations with 181

countries.

14:中国始终坚持维护世界和平、促进共同发展的外交政策的宗旨，致力于推动构建人类命运共同体

China has always upheld the purpose of its foreign policy of maintaining

world peace and promoting common development, and is committed to promoting

the building of a community with a shared future for mankind.

15:在和平共处五项原则的基础上，中国正在为建设和平、繁荣、和谐的世界作出更大的努力

Based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, China is making

greater efforts to build a peaceful, prosperous and harmonious world.

16:太极拳是一种武术，也是一种健身运动，在中国有着悠久的历史

Tai Chi is a kind of martial art and also a kind of fitness exercise, which

has a long history in China.

17:太极拳动作缓慢而柔和,适合任何年龄、性别、体型(build)的人

Tai Chi movements are slow and gentle, and are suitable for people of any

age, gender and build.

18:太极拳既可用于防身(self - defense),又能强身健体,因而深受中国人民和世界人民的喜爱

Tai Chi can be used for self-defense and can also build up one’s health.

Therefore, it is deeply loved by the Chinese people and people all over the

world.
